MyNotesHA1 privacy policy

introduction:

MyNotesHA1 is a secure tool for writing and organizing notes on your mobile device. The application adheres to the highest standards of privacy, and does not collect any personal data from users.

Data collected by the application:

MyNotesHA1 does not collect any personal data from users. All data is stored on your mobile device only, including:

Notes: All notes you enter into the app are stored on an internal Sqlite database local to your device.

Data backups: When you take a backup of your notes, they are saved as a local file on your device.

Other files: You can choose to export your notes as text files or HTML reports, which are saved as local files on your device.

How the data is used:

View Notes: Data is used to display the notes you have entered into the app.

Search for notes: Data is used to search for notes you have entered in the app.

Backup and restore: Data is used to back up and restore your notes.

Export Notes: Data is used to export your notes as text files or HTML reports.

With whom data is shared:

No data is shared with any person or third party. All data is stored on your mobile device only.

User options:

View Notes: You can view all the notes you have entered in the app.

Edit Notes: You can edit any note you entered in the app.

Delete Notes: You can delete any note you have entered in the app.

Take backups: You can take a backup of your notes and save it as a local file on your device.

Restore backups: You can restore a backup copy of your notes from a local file on your device.

Export notes: You can export your notes as text files or HTML reports.

Our commitments:

We are committed to protecting the privacy of your data.

We will not collect any personal data from you without your consent.

We will not share any data with any person or third party without your consent.

We will protect your data from unauthorized access or illegal use.

MyExpensesHA1 privacy policy

introduction:

MyExpensesHA1 is a secure tool for writing and organizing expenses on your mobile device. The application adheres to the highest standards of privacy, and does not collect any personal data from users.

Data collected by the application:

MyExpensesHA1 does not collect any personal data from users. All data is stored on your mobile device only, including:

Revenue and expenses: All revenue and expenses you enter into the app are stored on an internal Sqlite database local to your device.

Data backups: When you take a backup of your expenses, they are saved as a local file on your device.

Other files: You can choose to export your expenses as text files or HTML reports, which are saved as local files on your device.

How the data is used:

View expenses: Data is used to display the expenses you have entered into the app.

Search for expenses: Data is used to search for expenses you have entered in the app.

Backup and restore: Data is used to back up and restore your expenses.

Export revenue and expenses: Data is used to export your expenses as text files or HTML reports.

With whom data is shared:

No data is shared with any person or third party. All data is stored on your mobile device only.

User options:

View revenue and expenses: You can view all the revenue and expenses you have entered in the app.

Edit revenue and expenses: You can edit any revenue and expenses you entered in the app.

Delete revenue and expenses: You can delete any revenue and expenses you have entered in the app.

Take backups: You can take a backup of your revenue and expenses and save it as a local file on your device.

Restore backups: You can restore a backup copy of your revenue and expenses from a local file on your device.

Export expenses: You can export your revenue and expenses as text files or HTML reports.

Our commitments:

We are committed to protecting the privacy of your data.

We will not collect any personal data from you without your consent.

We will not share any data with any person or third party without your consent.

We will protect your data from unauthorized access or illegal use.
